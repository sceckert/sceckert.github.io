Projects

The Indexical Imagination: Documentary Aesthetics and the Literary History of Information Culture

My digital senior honors thesis examines literary information devices––indexes, tables of contents, footnotes, appendixes, etc––and their role in the social and literary production of knowledge.

The Early Novels Database

I designed the web interface for a bibliographic database of slow metadata on eighteenth century novels designed to re-imagine the role of the bibliographic database as a site for creative exploration and information dissemation.

Researching Benjamin Researching: The Arcades Project Through the Eyes of a Digital Flâneur

Using the form of a digital "assemblage" I created a web-essay that highlights the relationship between the form and content of the research practices of German philosopher Walter Benjamin in his massive, unfinished experiment in hisotoriography, The Arcades Project.
